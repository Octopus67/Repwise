## Long Description

Repwise is the all-in-one training and nutrition app built for lifters who want to get stronger, build muscle, and stay consistent.

SMART WORKOUT TRACKING
Log sets, reps, and weight with one tap. Repwise tracks your volume, detects personal records automatically, and shows your progress over time with clean analytics.

AI-POWERED NUTRITION
Scan barcodes or search from 200,000+ foods. Set calorie and macro targets, track meals throughout the day, and get a weekly nutrition score that adapts to your goals.

RECOVERY & READINESS
Connect Apple Health to pull HRV, resting heart rate, and sleep data. Repwise calculates a daily recovery score so you know when to push hard and when to back off.

PROGRESS ANALYTICS
Visualize your strength gains with volume charts, PR tracking, and body composition trends. Compare sessions side-by-side to see exactly what's working.

SOCIAL & COMPETE
Follow friends, share workouts, and climb weekly leaderboards. Earn badges for streaks, PRs, and milestones.

BUILT FOR SERIOUS LIFTERS
- Progressive overload tracking
- RPE and RIR logging
- Rest timer with auto-start
- Workout templates and routines
- Body stats and progress photos
- Data export (CSV)
- Dark mode throughout
- Works offline
