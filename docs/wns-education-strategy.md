# WNS Education & Engagement Strategy

## The Problem

**Your moat:** Goal-aware Weekly Net Stimulus algorithm that adjusts volume recommendations based on calorie balance, frequency, and intensity.

**User perception:** "It's just another workout tracker."

**Gap:** Users don't understand WHY Repwise is different or WHY they should log everything.

---

## Strategy: Progressive Disclosure + Proof Points

### Phase 1: Onboarding (Plant the Seed)

**Step 6.5: TDEE Reveal → Add "Smart Training" Teaser**

After showing the TDEE breakdown, add a new step explaining that training adapts too.

### Phase 2: First Workout (Show, Don't Tell)

Modal after first workout explaining Hypertrophy Units and what they earned.

### Phase 3: Analytics Tab (Educate)

"How It Works" card explaining WNS vs traditional set counting.

### Phase 4: Volume Pills (Real-Time Feedback)

Show adjusted targets during workouts with explanations.

### Phase 5: Weekly Report (Proof of Intelligence)

"Volume Intelligence" section showing goal-adjusted recommendations.

### Phase 6: Goal Change Notification

Alert when volume targets adjust due to goal change.

### Phase 7: Comparison Marketing

App store description and landing page highlighting the adaptive algorithm.

### Phase 8: Gamification

Achievements for optimal volume, consistency streaks, insight cards.

---

## Implementation Priority

**Week 1 (High Impact, Low Effort):**
1. Volume pills show adjusted targets during workout
2. Weekly report explains the adjustment
3. Add "How It Works" card to Analytics tab

**Week 2-4:** Onboarding teaser, first workout modal, goal change notifications, marketing copy, gamification.
