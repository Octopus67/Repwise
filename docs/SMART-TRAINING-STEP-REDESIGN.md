# SmartTrainingStep Redesign - Product Perspective

## Current Issues

1. **Generic emoji** - 🔥 for cutting feels cliché
2. **Bullet points are vague** - "15% less volume" means nothing to users
3. **No visual hierarchy** - Everything has equal weight
4. **"Why This Matters" is defensive** - Sounds like we're justifying ourselves
5. **Science badge feels like marketing** - Users don't care about citations yet
6. **No clear value proposition** - What do I actually GET?

---

## Redesigned Step 7: Smart Training

### New Messaging Strategy

**Core insight:** Users don't care about "volume" or "recovery capacity" - they care about **results without burnout**.

### Key Changes

1. **Better emojis:**
   - 💪 for workouts (action-oriented)
   - 🎯 for sets (target/goal)
   - ⚡ for dynamic adjustment (energy/smart)
   - ✨ for differentiation (special)

2. **Concrete numbers:**
   - "3-4 workouts per week" (not "volume")
   - "12-16 sets per muscle" (specific range)
   - Based on THEIR input (3 sessions/week)

3. **Benefits-focused:**
   - "Optimized for fat loss while preserving muscle"
   - "Adjusted weekly based on YOUR data"
   - "Never changes" vs "Adjusts every week"

4. **Remove:**
   - Science citations (save for settings/learn section)
   - Comparison grid (overwhelming, not needed)
   - "Why This Matters" defensive section
   - Technical jargon (volume, recovery capacity, stimulus)

5. **Add:**
   - Clear visual cards with icons
   - Specific numbers from their input
   - Contrast with "other apps"
   - Confidence-building language
