### Description

Repwise is the all-in-one training and nutrition app built for lifters who want to grow muscle using real science, not guesswork.

If you're like most serious lifters, your phone is a mess. One app for tracking workouts. Another for logging food. A spreadsheet for programming. Maybe a notes app for body measurements. You're stitching together five tools that were never designed to talk to each other, and you're losing data in the gaps.

Repwise fixes that. Everything lives in one place: your training, your nutrition, your progress, your coaching. And it's free.

Your workouts are tracked in hypertrophy units, a metric that accounts for rep range, proximity to failure, and stretch position so you can see exactly how much growth stimulus you're actually generating. No more guessing whether your session was productive. Repwise tells you.

Volume landmarks show you where each muscle group sits relative to your minimum effective volume, maximum adaptive volume, and maximum recoverable volume. You'll know when you're doing too little, when you're in the sweet spot, and when you're digging a recovery hole. Fatigue scoring tracks your accumulated systemic and local fatigue across the week so you can push hard without burning out.

On the nutrition side, you get a 3M+ food database and barcode scanning that makes logging meals fast and painless. Your TDEE adapts weekly based on your actual weight trend and intake, not some static calculator you used once and forgot about. Whether you're in a gaining phase, a cut, or maintaining, Repwise adjusts with you.

Every week, you get an intelligence report that breaks down what happened: which muscle groups got enough stimulus, which ones fell short, how your nutrition tracked against your targets, and what to adjust going forward. It's like having a coach review your week, except it happens automatically.

Repwise gives you four coaching modes. Full Auto handles your programming and nutrition targets entirely. Semi Auto lets you customize your split while the app manages volume and load progression. Manual mode gives you full control with real-time feedback on your metrics. And Coaching mode connects you with a real human coach for 1-on-1 guidance.

The training methodology is grounded in peer-reviewed hypertrophy research from scientists like Brad Schoenfeld and Mike Israetel. This isn't bro science repackaged in a pretty UI. The volume landmarks, fatigue management, and stimulus quantification are all rooted in published literature on muscle growth.

Progress photos with consistent lighting prompts and side-by-side comparisons let you see changes your scale can't capture. Because the number on the scale is only part of the story.

Here's the best part: Repwise is free. All the tracking, all the intelligence, all four coaching modes, the entire food database, barcode scanning, everything. The only paid feature is 1-on-1 human coaching at $9.99/mo for lifters who want personalized guidance from a real coach.

Stop juggling apps. Stop guessing. Start training with precision.

Download Repwise and see what your training has been missing.
