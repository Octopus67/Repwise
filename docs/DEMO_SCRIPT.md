## [0:30] DASHBOARD — show the app

"This is Repwise. I've been using it for about 4 months now,
so there's real data in here.

These rings up top — that's my calories, protein, carbs, fat
for today versus my targets. And the cool part is these targets
aren't just some generic number. The app calculates them based

"And this is my trend weight. So instead of panicking because

## [0:55] LOG FOOD — tap Log Food

"Logging food is super quick. We've got like 200,000 foods
in the database."

→ Tap Log Food → search "chicken" → tap result

"And here's what's different — every food has full micronutrient

## [1:15] TRAINING — tap Log tab

"Okay training — this is where it gets really fun."

→ Tap Log tab → show session list → tap into a recent session

"So when I'm in the gym logging my sets, the app shows me what
I did last time right here. So I know exactly what I need to
beat today.

But the real magic is — the app doesn't just count how many
sets I did. It actually calculates how much growth stimulus
each muscle got. Because here's the thing — a lazy set where
you're just going through the motions? That barely does anything.
A hard set where you're really pushing? That's where the growth
happens. And the app knows the difference."
